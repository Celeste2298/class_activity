What are Pueblos Magicos, you might ask?

Well for starters, it's literal translation is Magic Towns. Therefore, we can inquire that these towns bring in a bit of magic to those who choose to visit them.

If you are still wondering as to why they are given this title of being known as a Magic Town well it is actually granted by the Mexico Government to towns that exhibit a so called magical environment per say. This being through the towns beauty, craftsmenship/artesenal, or their cultural history of the town.
